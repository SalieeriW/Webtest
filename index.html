It's also important other tags like the nav, header, footer, aside. Where:

  • Header stands for the header (xd), its just top part of a page, usually where we put the logos and the nav tags.

    Example:
    I am a header, even thought I'm not situated at the top of the site.
  • Footer stands for footer, the lowest part of a page, where we usually put the copyright and bla bla bla...

    Example:
    I shouldn't be here, but anyways: All rights belongs to ©Nobody.
  • The nav stand for navigated, as we can guess, it the top part of a page thats lets us navigate to diferent parts of a website.

    Example:
    I'm captain Sparrow, click me to navigate.
  • The last thing is aside, just some facts that is not very related to the topic that we are talking.

    Example:
    I like watching anime and eat popcorns while I'm having a shower.
